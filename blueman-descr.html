Blueman

A thousand years ago, the Earth, a peaceful place, where Blueman was the king, lived a fearful time when Redman decided to dethrone him in hopes of getting his power, he comes to the castle and fights against our king's army and kills them and half of the kingdom's population without hesitation.

To base ourselves on the creation of the character of Blueman and his name was the result of a joke in class that we decided to implement in this video game. The character has adventurer characteristics and reference to his name "BLUEMAN" all of his clothes are blue. #chista
